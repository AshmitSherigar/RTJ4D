Atlas

The Titan Who Carried the Heavens

In Greek mythology, Atlas was condemned to hold up the sky for eternity. His story symbolizes endurance, strength, and the weight of responsibility. Despite his punishment, he remains a powerful figure in history and art, inspiring countless works and interpretations.

The Burden of Atlas

The image of Atlas carrying the heavens has been reimagined in various cultures, often representing the struggle of those who bear immense challenges. His tale serves as a reminder of perseverance and the unyielding spirit of those who carry the weight of the world.
